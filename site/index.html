Before booking

Questions before booking a fit call.

Short answers about access, the audit, scope, and migration risk.

Do I need to share access before the first call?

No. The first call is low-access. No Bubble export, admin or editor access, credentials, secrets, or production customer data are needed for the first conversation.

What access is needed for the scoping audit?

After a fit call, AppRebase needs agreed Bubble editor access, with read-only access acceptable where possible, and the Bubble export file. Access is requested only after audit scope, handling rules, AI-assisted analysis disclosure, and consent are clear.

Why do you audit before giving a fixed quote?

A live Bubble app cannot be priced precisely enough for a fixed-scope quote and timeline from screenshots or a quick call. The audit combines custom AI tooling, export analysis, manual app review, and founder context to agree what is in scope, what is excluded, what should wait, and what the migration will cost.

What do I receive after the audit?

The client-facing output is the agreed migration scope, fixed price, and timeline. AppRebase may create internal architecture notes, build specifications, or implementation plans for delivery, but those are not audit handover documents unless a later paid agreement explicitly includes them.

Does the audit obligate me to continue?

No. The audit is required before AppRebase can prepare a responsible migration quote, but it does not obligate you to approve implementation. If the budget, timing, scope, or handling rules do not fit, you can stop there.

Will you rebuild everything exactly as it exists in Bubble?

No. The goal is to rebuild the product behavior, screens, data, and workflows users rely on. Old Bubble clutter, obsolete fields, unused features, and accidental workarounds do not need to be copied.

Can you rebuild wrapped Bubble mobile apps?

Yes, if mobile is part of the agreed scope. AppRebase can rebuild the mobile experience as a native or Flutter app instead of preserving a wrapped Bubble app. The exact web or mobile stack is chosen during scoping based on the product, roadmap, budget, and handover needs.

Can you guarantee zero downtime or no data loss?

Not before the app is understood. The responsible commitment is to identify continuity, data, auth, workflow, validation, and cutover risks before implementation, then scope the migration around what has to stay safe.
